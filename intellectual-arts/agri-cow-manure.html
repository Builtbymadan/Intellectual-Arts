Description

Utkarsh Cow Dung Manure is 100% natural, organic composted manure sourced exclusively from Desi Indian cows. It enriches soil with nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium (NPK), and essential micronutrients. Regular application improves water retention, enhances microbial activity, and creates a natural shield against soil-borne diseases.

How to Use

  • Loosen top soil up to 2–3 inches
  • Apply manure near the root zone
  • Mix lightly with top soil
  • Water immediately after application
